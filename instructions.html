Instructions

1. You will need to register latest by 5th February 2019

2. Mandatory requirement for registration :

a. Bio of every participant

b. Description in not more than 30 words

c. Presentation about your Idea

d. Execution Plan(Optional)

3. The team size to participate in this hackathon can be an individual or upto 4 members

4. 25 teams will be invited for the event

5. 10-12 teams will present during the hack day to a Jury, after reviewing the progress after 24 hours

6. The winners will be evaluated based on the Evaluation Criteria

7. Please choose one of the themes, i.e. Water Quality or Location Intelligence as the base of your idea

8. In case you are located outside Bangalore and would like to participate in this event, you will be responsible for your travel and accommodation.

9. All the logistics including food, security, space will be provided at the AppDynamics office

10. Please carry your own laptop for the event
